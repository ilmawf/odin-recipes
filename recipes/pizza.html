Pizza

Homemade pizza is a delightful and customizable dish that starts with a simple, fresh dough made from flour, water, yeast, and olive oil. The dough is kneaded, allowed to rise, and then rolled out to the desired thickness. You can choose a thick, chewy crust or a thin, crispy one. After preparing the base, it's topped with a rich tomato sauce, a generous layer of mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings—such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, or onions—before being baked to golden perfection in a hot oven.

The beauty of homemade pizza lies in its versatility and the ability to experiment with different flavors and ingredients. You can make it healthier by using whole wheat flour for the dough or add a gourmet twist by using fresh herbs, premium cheeses, or unique toppings like arugula, prosciutto, or roasted vegetables. Each pizza is unique, and the homemade version offers a balance of crispness, softness, and an abundance of fresh flavors that make it a satisfying meal f or any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. fresh prepared pizza dough
  • 1 cup marinara
  • 2 oz. Canadian bacon slices
  • 1 oz. pepperoni slices
  • 1 small (3 to 4 oz.) red bell pepper
  • 1 small (3 to 4 oz.) green bell pepper
  • 1 small (3 to 4 oz.) red onion
  • 1 (2 1/4-oz.) can sliced black olives
  • 6 oz. low-moisture part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup torn fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Place a pizza stone or baking sheet on middle rack in oven. Preheat oven to 500°F (do not remove pizza stone while oven preheats).
  2. Roll dough out into a 14-inch circle on a lightly floured work surface. Transfer dough circle to a piece of parchment paper.
  3. Spread marinara over dough, leaving a 1-inch border. Top with Canadian bacon, pepperoni, red and green bell peppers, red onion, black olives, and mozzarella.
  4. Transfer parchment with pizza to hot pizza stone. Bake at 500°F until cheese is bubbly and lightly browned, 9 to 10 minutes. Remove pizza, and let stand 5 minutes; sprinkle with Parmesan and basil.
